Fried Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Oil for frying (vegetable or peanut oil)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, marinate chicken pieces in buttermilk for at least 1 hour, or overnight for best results.
  2. In another bowl, combine flour, salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  3. Heat oil in a large skillet or deep fryer to 350°F (175°C).
  4. Remove chicken pieces from buttermilk, letting excess drip off. Coat each piece thoroughly in the seasoned flour mixture.
  5. Carefully place chicken in hot oil, a few pieces at a time, and fry until golden brown and cooked through, about 15-20 minutes depending on size.
  6. Remove chicken from oil and let drain on paper towels. Serve hot and enjoy!
