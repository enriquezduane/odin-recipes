Cheesecake

Description

Cheesecake is a rich and creamy dessert consisting of a crumbly crust topped with a smooth, velvety filling. It's a classic treat enjoyed worldwide for its indulgent texture and delightful flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 pound cream cheese, softened
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together sugar and flour. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth. Gradually add sugar mixture, beating until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla, sour cream, heavy cream, and salt until well blended.
  4. Pour filling into prepared crust. Bake in preheated oven for 55 to 60 minutes, or until center is set. Allow to cool completely before removing from pan. Chill in refrigerator for at least 4 hours before serving.
